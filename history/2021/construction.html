Welcome To

Wim Corporation PTY LTD

We don't just construct buildings, we build quality of life.

Wim Corporation PTY LTD (WC), is a 100% Black Owned Private Company founded in 2017 February, by Constance Busisiwe Mmutloane who is a 60% shareholder and William Ralesabe Mmutloane who is a 40% shareholder. With their expertise in many spheres of Construction & Architecture, the directors from the onset steered WC to successfully provide excellent services to various clients in the Mahikeng, Lichtenburg and Sanieshof areas. This included small and large scale projects, repair and alteration projects, ranging from residential to commercial sectors.


Combined, the directors have over 20yrs of experience in the construction sector, which makes the company to be rich in service and skill. Every project is closely monitored by them by being on site and assuring each client of a satisfying end product every time. WC's efficient, trained team of artisans, as well as its internal quality assurance processes, ensures that all projects meet customer requirements and specifications to perfection.
